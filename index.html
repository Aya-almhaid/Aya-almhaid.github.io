01. About Me

I'm a full-stack developer who enjoys turning real-world workflows into clean, usable software. Most of my recent work has centered on clinic & healthcare management systems — building multi-role platforms (admin, doctor, receptionist, patient) with secure authentication, appointment scheduling, medical records, and billing.

I care about getting the details right: proper role-based access control, validated APIs, and interfaces that stay simple even as features grow. I'm comfortable across the stack — from designing a PostgreSQL schema to shipping a polished React + MUI interface on top of it.

I also have a background in networking (CCNA-certified), which shapes how I think about systems — client/server communication, request flow, and troubleshooting connectivity issues end to end.
